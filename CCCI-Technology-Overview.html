The Modules

A capability-level view of each layer — what every part of the platform does for members, chapters and the World Council.

1

Public Platform & Gateway

The front door

Movement Site

The brand home and narrative — the public face of CCCI as a global movement, built to attract the right people and let the wrong ones self-select out.

Application & Vetting Gateway

The structured intake for membership across every tier, feeding the vetting workflow.

Visitor Registration

The guest pathway — register, attend, experience the room before joining.

International Directory

A classified, searchable directory of member businesses across the network.

Events & Summit Portal

Discovery, registration and ticketing for chapter events and the annual Summit.

Purpose & Content Hub

The movement's content home — purpose-led, next-generation, built for the platforms they live on.

2

Member Super-App

Web · iOS · Android

Profiles & Digital Credential

Verified member profiles and a digital membership credential — identity and standing in one place.

Meeting Management

The weekly meeting, organised — agenda, check-in, attendance and continuity.

The Forum

Confidential peer-advisory circles — scheduling, membership and private discussion spaces.

The Deal Room

A discreet opportunity exchange with structured matchmaking and a due-diligence workflow.

Introductions Engine

Curated, discreet member-to-member introductions — the network, made navigable.

Recognition & Honours

A merit-based system that recognises genuine contribution — never quotas.

The Academy

Masterclasses and learning — a structured library and programme delivery.

Messaging & Engagement

Secure messaging, notifications and the everyday connective tissue of the network.

3

Chapter Console

For chairs & directors

Chapter Dashboard

The operational cockpit — the health and activity of a chapter at a glance.

Membership Lifecycle

From application to onboarding to renewal — the full member journey, managed.

Attendance & Visitors

Weekly attendance, visitor tracking and the growth pipeline they create.

Dues & Renewals

Billing, collection and renewal management at the chapter level.

Pipeline & Growth

Prospect and visitor pipeline tracking that drives consistent chapter growth.

Local Events & Analytics

Event management and the performance metrics that keep standards consistent.

4

HQ & Governance Console

The World Council

Global Membership Oversight

The institution's complete membership picture across every chapter and country.

Franchise Administration

Master, regional and chapter franchise management — the engine of replication.

Finance & Royalty Engine

Global financial flows and the royalty model that lets HQ earn on the whole network.

Ethics & Grievance Cases

Independent case management for the ethics charter — governance with teeth.

Global Analytics

Intelligence and reporting across the entire institution, in one view.

Brand & Content Governance

Centralised control of brand and content, deployed consistently everywhere.

03

Engineering Principles

The standards the build is held to — the qualities that let a single platform run a global institution.

i
Centralised core, localised deliveryBuilt once, deployed to every region — shared brand and data, local content and currency.
ii
Mobile-first, multi-platformA first-class experience on web, iOS and Android for members on the move.
iii
Cloud-native & scalableDesigned to grow from one chapter to a body in fifty countries without re-building.
iv
Multi-tenant for franchiseEvery chapter and country isolated yet unified — the structure replication demands.
v
API-first & integration-readyBuilt to connect — payments, identity, communications and future services.
vi
Enterprise-grade security & privacyMember data protected to the standard this audience expects and requires.
04

What Phase 1 Delivers

The module map above defines what the platform does. The complete technical architecture — the how — is the core deliverable of the first phase, and the foundation everything is built on.

Phase 1 · Technical Discovery & Architecture

The blueprint that makes the build correct, scalable and future-proof.

This is the time-intensive, foundational work that must be done properly before development begins. It turns the module map into a buildable system — and it is where the real engineering decisions are made.

  • Detailed system & service architecture
  • Data models & schema design
  • API contracts & integration specifications
  • Security, privacy & compliance architecture
  • Scalability & infrastructure design
  • Technology stack finalisation
  • Build roadmap, estimates & phasing
  • Prototype & technical proof-of-concept
In short: this overview shows the scope and ambition of the system. Getting it built correctly — architected to scale across a global franchise without costly rework later — is precisely the work of Phase 1, and the reason it should be planned carefully with the team that designed it.
